--- title: Articles description: This is an example of how to markup an article using Jekyll so that Google can enhance your appearance in search results. headline: Article Headline article_thumbnail_image: placehold.it/800x800 thumbnail_image_width: 800 thumbnail_image_height: 800 published_date: 2015-02-05 modified_date: 2015-02-06 author: John Doe publisher: Google publisher_logo_image: placehold.it/600x60 logo_image_width: 600 logo_image_height: 60 article_description: A most wonderful article. _comments: headline: The headline of the article. Headlines should not exceed 110 characters. article_thumbnail_image: The representative image of the article. thumbnail_image_width: The height of the image, in pixels. thumbnail_image_height: The width of the image, in pixels. published_date: The date the article was first published. modified_date: The date the article was last modified. author: The author of the article. publisher: The name of the publisher. publisher_logo_image: The URL pointing to the logo of the publisher. logo_image_width: The width of the logo, in pixels. logo_image_height: The height of the logo, in pixels. article_description: A short description of the article. ---
